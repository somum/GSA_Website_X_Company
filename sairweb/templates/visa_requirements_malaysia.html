Check List
For Tourist visa in Malaysia, the requirements are:
  • Passport (Original & Photocopy, Validity Minimum 7 Months)
  • 1 Visa form
  • Photo 2 copies (35/50 Recent)
  • Bank Statement with Solvency certificate(Original (180 Days) Last six months)
  • Trade license- Translated & Notary ( for -Businessman)
  • Student ID Card & Birth Certificate (for-Student & Child)
  • Office ID Card Copy
  • Visiting Card
  • Nikah Nama & Marriage Certificate ( First Time Travel With Wife)
  • Work Permit (For Foreigner)
  • Return Air Ticket (Photocopy)
  • Hotel Reservation
  • Official Forwarding letter (Letter Pad / NOC)

For Business visa in Malaysia, the extra requirements are:
  • Letter from Work
  • Business Card

Visa application form completely filled in with original sign as per passport.
