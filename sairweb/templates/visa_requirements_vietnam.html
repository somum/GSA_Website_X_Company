Check List
For Tourist visa in Vietnam, the requirements are:
  • Original Passport
  • 1 Visa form
  • Photo 2 copies (35/45 Recent)
  • Passport Copy
  • Flight itinerary
  • Medical Appointment & Local Doctor Suggestion Letter for Medical Visa
  • Personal Bank statement With Solvency Chartificate
  • Copy of previous visit to at least 2 other countries

For Business visa in Vietnam, the extra requirements are:
  • Letter from Work
  • Business Card
  • Work ID Card

Consular officers reserve the rights to request additional documents as deemed necessary.
