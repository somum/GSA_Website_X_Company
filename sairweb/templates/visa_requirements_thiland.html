Check List
For Tourist visa in Thiland, the requirements are:
  • Original Passport
  • 1 Visa form
  • Photo 2 copies (35/45 Recent)
  • Passport Copy
  • Personal Request Letter
  • Official Letter (Forwarding)
  • Medical Appointment & Local Doctor Suggestion Letter for Medical Visa
  • Student ID card (For- Student)
  • Business trade license (Translate & notary) (For- Businessman)
  • Personal Bank statement With Solvency Chartificate (Last 6 month. Balance: Single 60,000.00, family 120,000.00)
  • Visiting Card
  • Air ticket or Travel itinerary
  • Disclaimer Form

For Business visa in Thiland, the extra requirements are:
  • Letter from Thai Company
  • Thai Company Trade Registration-0401, Vat Certificate & Balance Sheet
